Contact Information

Uptown Vocal consistently enhances events with its fresh energy and smooth style. This charming and talented performance group is a perfect fit for any occasion! UV has impressed audiences at hundreds of events ranging from weddings and private parties to company banquets and charity galas. The group has performed at the White House, opened for Ben Folds, and been featured on national television. Whatever the size or scale, Uptown Vocal can make your event unforgettable.
